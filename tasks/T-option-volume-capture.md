# T-option-volume-capture — capture per-strike option volume (bid/ask already landed)

> **Source:** course transcript req #7
> (`documentation/transcripts/AlgoTradingCourse2-Greeks-et-strategies-vol.md` §6). Without
> volume per strike, the analytics stay "theoretical" — liquidity is what makes a strategy
> implementable.

## The gap
`MarketStateSnapshot` (`packages/infra/src/algotrading/infra/contracts/tables.py:~74`) carries
`bid`/`ask`/`last`/`spread_pct` (good — not the mid) but **no `volume`** on the option side;
volume exists only on `DailyBar` (underlying OHLC). The transcript wants per-strike volume.

## Scope
- Add a **`volume`** field to the option-side snapshot contract + the CP-REST capture
  (`market_fields.py` / close-capture) so per-strike liquidity is banked alongside bid/ask.
- Carry it through to the coverage panel / chain-quality readout (the data-quality face).
- Confirm the IBKR CP-REST field id for option volume before wiring (probe the gateway).

## Depends on / note
Contract change → universe/contract golden regenerated by design (ADR 0028). Pre-capture dev
change, no banked record carries the old shape.

## Done criteria
Option-side snapshot carries `volume`; CP-REST capture fills it; coverage surfaces it; goldens
regenerated; gate green.
