# Task — QC coverage to the blueprint: interpolate interior tenors, fall back at the edges

**Status:** queued (2026-06-16). **Owner ruling: Vincent — "follow the blueprint."**
**Decision of record:** [`ADR 0052`](../.agent/decisions/0052-qc-coverage-floors-to-blueprint-interpolate-and-fallback.md).
**Lane:** `infra-` (surface projection + QC) + `core-` (qc.yaml thresholds, hashed).

## Why

Post ADR-0051 the close capture is clean index-only, but SX5E still pages three CRITICAL QC checks
(`tenor_coverage_floor`, `delta_band_completeness`, `calendar_sanity`) — **only at the curve edges**
(`10d`, `2y`, `3y` LEAPs) and on a ~2e-4 short-end calendar wiggle. The liquid core (`1m`–`18m`)
passes. The blueprint says those edges should be **interpolated (Eq. 22)** or **marked
low-confidence/unusable** (`05-math-notes`), coverage is a **≥95% ratio over monitored maturities**
(`14-slos`), and calendar/arb checks flag only **gross** pathologies (`02-math-framework`). The
current checks hardcode `severity=CRITICAL` over a flat per-tenor floor → a false-critical that
makes every close untrustworthy. See ADR 0052 for the full blueprint citations.

## Evidence (2026-06-16, run `9a3d7ac…`, index-only)

- `tenor_coverage_floor` SX5E breach: `{10d: 0, 2y: 0, 3y: 0}` vs floor 5; `1m/3m/6m/12m/18m` pass.
- `delta_band_completeness` SX5E: edge tenors `too_few_points` / `*_edge_unreached`; core has 26–31.
- `calendar_sanity` SX5E: `w_long=1.62e-3` vs `w_short=1.87e-3` at the short end — noise, not gross.

## Scope of change

1. **`infra/surfaces` projection — fill interior pinned tenors by Eq.-22 variance interpolation.**
   A pinned tenor between two liquid monitored maturities is interpolated in total-variance space
   (`02-math-framework` Eq. 22); it does not need its own captured points. Emit a confidence /
   `source = interpolated|direct|extrapolated` label per projected tenor so QC and the front can see
   how each pin was produced.
2. **`infra/surfaces` projection — edge tenors are a labelled fallback.** A pinned tenor requiring
   extrapolation beyond the liquid range (e.g. `2y/3y` past `18m`, `10d` below the front) is marked
   `low_confidence` / `unusable` per `05-math-notes` — not silently dropped, not a hard zero that
   reads as a defect.
3. **`infra/qc/checks.py` — `check_tenor_coverage_floor`** (`:544`): evaluate on the post-
   interpolation surface; an interpolated interior pin is covered. Demote an **extrapolated edge**
   breach from `SEVERITY_CRITICAL` to `WARNING`. Keep a within-liquid-range floor + a ≥95%
   monitored-coverage ratio (`14-slos`) as the CRITICAL teeth.
4. **`infra/qc/checks.py` — `check_delta_band_completeness`** (`:595`): same interior/edge split —
   full ±30Δ band CRITICAL only where the surface is liquid; partial band on extrapolated edges is a
   WARNING.
5. **`infra/qc/checks.py` — `check_calendar_sanity`** (`:391`): page CRITICAL only on a **material**
   variance inversion — add an absolute/relative tolerance and an ultra-short-maturity guard so
   sub-threshold noise is at most WARNING. Eq. 21 stays the diagnostic.
6. **`core` / `configs/qc.yaml`** (hashed, ADR 0028): replace the flat `tenor_floors` semantics with
   {within-liquid floor, monitored-coverage ratio, calendar tolerance, ultra-short floor}. Regenerate
   the `qc` config-hash golden once. `QcThresholdConfig` typed home updated to match.
7. **Front (optional, follow-up):** surface the per-tenor `source`/confidence label on the coverage
   panel so an interpolated/extrapolated pin reads honestly (ties into the dropped coverage column,
   [[blueprint-return-dispersion-diagnostic]] #7).

## Acceptance

- SX5E close capture: no CRITICAL from edge illiquidity; `2y/3y/10d` show as interpolated or
  low-confidence/unusable, not failures. A genuine liquid-core coverage collapse still pages CRITICAL.
- `calendar_sanity` green on the 2e-4 short-end wiggle; still red on a material inversion (test both).
- `qc` config-hash golden regenerated by design; section isolation holds (only `qc` moves).
- Gate green. No capture-path change (derived-surface QC + projection only).

## Out of scope

Advanced no-arbitrage surface repair (full butterfly/calendar enforcement) — later release per
`02-math-framework`. This task realigns the *basic* diagnostics + coverage verdict only.
